The Paradise of North Sumatera

Please be advised that this is not an actual UI/UX project; rather, it serves as my initial experimentation with Figma. I have endeavored to utilize prototype animation in its projection. 'The Paradise Of North Sumatra' denotes a website aimed at reviewing the scenic splendor of North Sumatra, and my aim was to craft its UI/UX within Figma as part of this experimental process. I reiterate that this endeavor is solely an experiment and does not represent a formal or serious project in conventional terms.
